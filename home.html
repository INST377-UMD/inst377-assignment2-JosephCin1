Audio Navigation Instructions

Use the Turn On Listening button to enable voice commands for navigating the page. Make sure your microphone is enabled (check the top-left of your browser).

  • Navigate to a different page by saying: "Navigate to <page>"
  • Change the page background color by saying: "Change the color to <color>"
  • Have the site respond with "Hello World" by saying: "Hello"
